The Best Movies According to Caroline

My Top Three Movies at this Current Moment in Time

Barefoot Gen

This film is an emotional rollarcoaster told from the perspective of a child who survived Hiroshima.

Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse

This film has an beautiful mix of artistic styles that reflect the character's emotional states and connection with others. I also enjoyed SunSpider's cameo.

Get Out

This twists, turns and critique of society in this film was chilling in all the best ways.
